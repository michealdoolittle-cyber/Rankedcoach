Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

RankedCoach provides match review, improvement tracking, and coaching-oriented analytics for competitive players. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

RankedCoach is intended for personal performance review, self-reflection, and user-authorized coaching workflows. Riot-linked access is opt-in and limited to the features you choose to use.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide, such as email address and login credentials handled through Supabase authentication.
  • Profile details you enter in the app, such as Riot ID, preferred region, profile names, theme settings, and accessibility preferences.
  • Gameplay-related records you import, create, or save, including match snapshots, reflections, goals, and coaching notes.
  • Technical information needed to operate the service, such as browser local storage, session data, and basic request logs from our hosting providers.

How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To save your progress and sync data across sessions or devices.
  • To generate dashboards, trends, summaries, and coaching insights based on the data you choose to store.
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the service.

Riot Account Data

RankedCoach is designed to support Riot-authorized account linking only after Riot approval and user opt-in. If Riot Sign On becomes available for this product, we will request your permission before accessing personal Riot account data and will only use that data to provide features you authorize.

Storage and Service Providers

We use third-party infrastructure providers to run RankedCoach. These may include Supabase for authentication and database storage, Cloudflare for hosting and delivery, and additional payment or communication services if enabled later.

Cookies and Local Storage

RankedCoach may use browser local storage or similar technologies to preserve settings, local app state, and session-related preferences. These technologies help the site function and improve usability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the app, and we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the integrity and security of the service.

Retention and Deletion

We keep account and app data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to operate the service. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support@rankedcoach.gg.

Your Choices

You can choose whether to sign in, whether to enter a Riot ID, and whether to use guest mode or synced features. If Riot Sign On is enabled later, you will also be able to choose whether to authorize account linking.

Children's Privacy

RankedCoach is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@rankedcoach.gg. Product feedback and bug reports can be sent to feedback@rankedcoach.gg. Business inquiries can be sent to business@rankedcoach.gg.
